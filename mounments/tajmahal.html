Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal 'Crown of the Palace',is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the southern bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor ShahJahan (reigned from 1628 to 1658)to house the tomb of his favorite wife,Mumtaz Mahal; it also houses the tomb of ShahJahan himself.The tomb is the centerpiece of a 17-hectare(42-acre)complex,which includes a mosque and aguesthouse, and is set in formal gardens bounded on three sides by a crenelated wall.
